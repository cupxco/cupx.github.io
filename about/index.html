About CupX Corporation

CupX Corporation is a student-led technology initiative focused on building practical software tools and academic systems while learning computer science through real-world implementation.

The goal of CupX is not just to create small utilities, but to develop structured, scalable systems such as academic management platforms, library systems, and functional web applications.

What CupX Represents

• Hands-on learning through real deployments
• Experimentation with web technologies
• Building structured academic tools
• Long-term growth into a professional technology brand

Founder

CupX was initiated as a learning-first project by a computer science student aiming to transform academic experimentation into structured software systems.
